.DISCUSSION...

Fire weather concerns continue to be limited as moisture
continues to move back into the area leading to gradually
increasing rain chances. Showers and thunderstorms will become
more area-wide Wednesday through the weekend. Over the next
7 days forecast rainfall totals vary from 4 to 7 inches across
northwestern areas to less than an inch for extreme southeast
Louisiana and much of the Mississippi coast. For most of the
area, for most of the week, minimum humidities are likely to be
60 percent or higher every day.

chance of a sea-breeze set up during ignitions?

Can't rule out patchy areas of fog, however, will be fully 
dependent on if the area sees rain today. If so, chances for 
patchy fog will be higher. But regardless, not anticipating 
widespread dense fog concerns. 

Doesn't appear to be a well-defined seabreeze today thanks to 
persistent/breezy SE winds. Rather, winds pick up mid/late morning
and remain out of the southeast through the afternoon/evening 
without any significant wind shift.
